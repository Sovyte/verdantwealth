Key Terms
NIFTY
NSE's benchmark index of 50 large-cap Indian stocks.
FII
Foreign Institutional Investors — overseas funds investing in Indian markets.
DII
Domestic Institutional Investors — Indian funds, LIC, mutual funds.
VaR
Value at Risk — statistical measure of potential portfolio loss.
MPC
RBI's Monetary Policy Committee — sets India's interest rates.
GSEC
Government Securities — India's sovereign bonds, key for debt allocation.
