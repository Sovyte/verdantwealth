# Verdant Wealth Co. — Solari Prime AI Suite

Verdant Wealth Co. is a premium financial intelligence platform designed specifically for Indian retirement planning and long-term wealth cultivation. It combines over 20 years of human advisory expertise from P. Saravana Kumar with a proprietary suite of AI models, led by the **Solari Prime** Auto-CoT engine.

---

## 🚀 Features

### 1. Solari Prime AI Analyzer
*   **Auto-CoT (Chain of Thought):** Uses advanced reasoning to break down complex market queries into logical steps (Macro, Technical, Sentiment, Risk, and Actionable Signals).
*   **Interactive Visualizations:** Includes AI-generated Mind Maps (Influence Networks), Portfolio Allocation Pie Charts, Sector Radar, and Global Impact Analysis.
*   **Retirement Focused:** Specialized in NIFTY/SENSEX analysis, RBI policy impacts, and FII/DII flow interpretation.

### 2. Live Market Intelligence
*   **Real-time Ticker:** Live-streaming data for NIFTY 50, BANK NIFTY, USD/INR, Gold, Silver, and major Indian equities via TwelveData API.
*   **Sector Momentum:** Real-time performance tracking of Indian industry sectors.
*   **World Analysis:** Tracking of global catalysts like Brent Crude, US Fed actions, and geopolitical events.

### 3. Client Advisory Portal
*   **Personalized Dashboard:** Secure access for clients to track their AI queries and schedule 1:1 sessions.
*   **Direct Advisory:** Seamless integration with Calendly for booking discovery calls.
*   **Secure Auth:** Powered by Supabase for encrypted user management and data storage.

---

## 🛠️ Technical Stack

*   **Frontend:** Vanilla HTML5, CSS3 (Glassmorphism UI), JavaScript (ES6+).
*   **Backend:** Python 3.x, Flask.
*   **AI Engine:** Groq (Llama 3.3 70B Model) for high-speed, structured JSON reasoning.
*   **Data APIs:** TwelveData (Market Quotes), Alpha Vantage (Sector Performance).
*   **Database:** SQLite (Local logs) & Supabase (User Auth/Profiles).
*   **Email:** SMTP integration for automated advisory enquiries and welcome sequences.
